Dice Town Online Rules

Objective

Get the most points before the nugget or property stocktpile is empty

Points

  • Dollars => each dollars give 0.5 points
  • Nuggets => each nugget gives 1 point
  • Sheriff => at the end of the game the player that is sheriff is granted 5 points
  • Properties => each property gives you points as indicated on the card
  • General Sorms => can give you points as indicated on the card

Game cycle

Until the game ends a cycle of dice throwing and results sequence continues

Dice throwing

Each player throws his dice and can choose the dices he wants to keep for the next round to build up his dice sequence. This cycle continues until at least on player has rolled and chosen his 5 dices, the next round all the remaining players will have all their remaining dices selected. The game then goes to the results phase

Results

The player

Draw

If two or more players are in a draw to win one of the categories, the sherif will decide who wins the category
